    Registration Opens Soon for 2023







    WiDS Kathmandu 2023 is an independent event that is organized by Manisha Panta and Reecha Khanal as part of the annual WiDS Worldwide conference and WiDS Datathon organized by Stanford University and an estimated 150+ locations worldwide, which features outstanding women doing outstanding work in the field of data science. All genders are invited to attend all WiDS Worldwide conference events.

    We are planning to organize WiDS Kathmandu Datathon in February, 2023 and WiDS Kathmandu Conference in June, 2023.
